Brief Synopsis of Services

Taxation

Direct Taxation

With specialization in providing highly effective and efficient Income tax services to individuals, small scale and medium scale businesses, we at K M R strive to provide customized solutions as per the needs and comfort of the clients. The clients have a direct interaction with the partners and seniors in the firm. The team is always ahead with adapting the on-going tax reforms. The firm also handles scrutiny assessments and our teams have a good liaison with Tax department.

Indirect Taxation:

The coverage of Indirect Taxation includes Goods and Service Tax [GST] under which we have helped clients to implement and comply with the law more effectively. GST being a comprehensive indirect tax on manufacture, sale and consumption of goods and services throughout India has replaced various taxes levied by the central and state governments. Our periodic review of the changeover and tax impacts of the new reforms helps our client to get assistance in analyzing business transactions, preparation of returns and complying with the law and its applicability to the client more efficiently.

Audit and Assurance:

A highly efficient team of qualified chartered accountants accompany in auditing and assurance services. Our audit and assurances service offerings are based on a complete understanding of the clients’ business specifics and industry peculiarities. The audit approach is based on the nature & requirement for audit and key issues involved. Our audit and assurance solutions range from Statutory Audits, Internal Audits, Tax Audits, Transfer Pricing Audits, Management Audits, Concurrent Audits, etc. The audit deliverables range from reporting for statutory compliances to enhancing the client’s business financial and operational performance.

Advisory and other Professional Services

RERA

The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 provides for establishing regulatory authorities at the state level to register residential real estate projects and seeks to regulate contracts in an efficient and transparent manner. Our team has been studying about all the aspects of the Act and has gained a vide experience in providing the services under the Act to the clients.

Company Law Matters

We ensure your company complies with its statutory obligations pro-actively and efficiently. Company secretarial services are essential for handling the numerous management tasks such as organizing documents, gathering information, keeping track of deadlines, analyzing reports.

Financial Advisory Services

We provide advisory services around Fund raising, M&A transactions, Capital restructurings, business modeling and forensic investigations.

Valuations

We offer a complete range of services for business valuation. No matter whether it's for a new start-up or for an established organization, our prime focus is to deliver our services with utmost quality and in a most ethical and independent manner.

FDI and Other Foreign transaction

In the era of globalization, we provide seamless and integrated services to facilitate your business expansion. For this purpose understanding and compliance with laws of land and International business laws is essential. We offer our services in adherence with FEMA rules & regulations and other applicable laws.
